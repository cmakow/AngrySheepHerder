Angry Sheep Herder

The rules are simple!

  • Click as many sheep as you can! The score will go up as you herd the sheep.
  • If a sheep shrinks to nothing, a red dot will appear and you will lose a life.
  • Last for as long as you can! When you have lost three lives, game over!
  • There is an accuracy rating - try to keep your accuracy as high as possible!
